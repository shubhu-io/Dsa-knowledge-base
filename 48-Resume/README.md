## Key Resume Principles

### The 6-Second Rule
Recruiters spend an average of 6-7 seconds on initial resume screening. Your resume must:
- Grab attention immediately
- Highlight relevant experience
- Show measurable results
- Be easy to scan

### ATS (Applicant Tracking System) Optimization
- 75% of resumes are rejected by ATS before human eyes
- Use keywords from job descriptions
- Avoid complex formatting
- Submit in proper file formats

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- Generic resumes for every application
- Listing responsibilities instead of achievements
- Ignoring keywords from job descriptions
- Poor formatting and readability
- Typos and grammatical errors
- Missing contact information
- Including irrelevant experience
- Using unprofessional email addresses
