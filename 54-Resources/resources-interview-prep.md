## Study Plans

### 3-Month Study Plan
```markdown
## Month 1: Fundamentals
- Week 1-2: Data Structures (Arrays, Linked Lists, Stacks, Queues)
- Week 3-4: Basic Algorithms (Sorting, Searching, Recursion)

## Month 2: Intermediate Topics
- Week 5-6: Trees and Graphs
- Week 7-8: Dynamic Programming

## Month 3: Advanced Topics and Practice
- Week 9-10: System Design Basics
- Week 11-12: Mock Interviews and Review
```

### Weekly Practice Schedule
```markdown
## Daily Practice
- Morning: 1 Easy/Medium problem (30-45 minutes)
- Afternoon: 1 Medium/Hard problem (60-90 minutes)
- Evening: Review and update notes (30 minutes)

## Weekly Goals
- Solve 10-15 problems
- Complete 1 system design problem
- 1 mock interview
- Review 1 algorithm concept
```

## Final Tips

### Before the Interview
1. Review common problems for the company
2. Practice explaining your thought process
3. Prepare questions for the interviewer
4. Test your technical setup

### During the Interview
1. Clarify requirements before coding
2. Think out loud
3. Start with brute force, then optimize
4. Test your solution with examples

### After the Interview
1. Reflect on your performance
2. Note areas for improvement
3. Follow up with a thank you note
4. Continue practicing
