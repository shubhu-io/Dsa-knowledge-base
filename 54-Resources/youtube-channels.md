## Video Watching Tips

### Active Learning

1. **Pause and code** - Don't just watch
2. **Take notes** - Write key concepts
3. **Rewatch** - If unclear, watch again
4. **Implement** - Code along with the video
5. **Practice** - Solve similar problems after

### Efficient Watching

1. **Speed:** Use 1.25x-1.5x speed
2. **Notes:** Keep a notebook ready
3. **Timestamps:** Jump to relevant sections
4. **Playlist:** Create study playlists
5. **Breaks:** Take breaks every 30 minutes
