## Study Tips

### Consistent Practice

Spend at least **30 minutes daily** on practice. Consistency beats marathon sessions.
Short, focused study periods lead to better retention and understanding.

### Active Recall

After reading a concept, close the documentation and try to explain it from memory.
This technique significantly improves long-term retention of complex topics.

### Spaced Repetition

Review previously learned topics at increasing intervals. Revisit a topic after one day,
then three days, then one week. This reinforces neural pathways and prevents forgetting.

### Code by Hand

Practice writing code on paper or a whiteboard. This builds muscle memory and helps
you think through solutions without relying on an IDE's autocomplete features.

### Teach What You Learn

Explaining concepts to others is one of the most effective ways to solidify your
understanding. Try writing explanations or discussing topics with peers.
